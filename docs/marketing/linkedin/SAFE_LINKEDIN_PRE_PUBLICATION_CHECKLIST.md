# SAFE LinkedIn Pre-Publication Checklist

> Run this checklist for every launch post before scheduling.

Last updated: 2026-05-15

---

## 1. Post Metadata

Complete before drafting:

- Date:
- Pillar: `P1`, `P2`, `P3`, `P4`, `P5`, or `P1/P5 transversal`
- Segment: `A established solo/small firm`, `B young lawyer launching`, or `A+B`
- Primary job: `profile view`, `comment`, `DM`, `newsletter`, `audit`, `quiz`, `demo`
- Primary CTA:
- Source claim IDs used:

---

## 2. Four Mandatory Checks

| Check | Pass criteria | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Voice SAFE | Operator voice, short lines, concrete details, no corporate filler | `[ ]` |
| CTA unique | One primary action; no competing audit/quiz/demo/newsletter asks | `[ ]` |
| Proof sourced | Every number appears in `SAFE_LINKEDIN_SOURCE_LEDGER.md` | `[ ]` |
| No exaggeration | No unsupported legal, compliance, ROI, or market guarantee | `[ ]` |

Do not schedule if any box fails.

---

## 3. Anti-Fiction Check

- `[ ]` No invented scene, timestamp, place, quantity, or dialogue.
- `[ ]` Any personal story detail is already in the approved founder-story facts or came from a documented interview.
- `[ ]` Any "j'ai vu" or "on m'a confirmé" statement stays inside the known facts.
- `[ ]` No dramatic embellishment was added to improve the post.

---

## 4. CTA Gate

- `[ ]` AUDIT is mentioned only for Segment A or established-firm pain.
- `[ ]` QUIZ is mentioned only if the quiz is live, scored, captures email, and delivers the promised result.
- `[ ]` Links are not placed in the post body if reach matters; use comment/profile/DM as appropriate.
- `[ ]` Booking link works if the post asks for a demo.

---

## 5. Week-1 Intent Check

Use this table for May 18-23:

| Date | Asset | Primary intent | Mandatory adjustment |
|---|---|---|---|
| May 18 | Arrival/founder story | Profile views + first follows | No lead magnet. |
| May 19 | Lawyer/QuickBooks story | Conversation + optional AUDIT DM | Keep story facts-only; source memory-loss claim or soften. |
| May 20 | 4 KPI carousel | Saves + newsletter/AUDIT DM | Verify every KPI number before export. |
| May 21 | Generic tools contrarian | Debate + newsletter | Replace absolute claims with safe claim language. |
| May 22 | Solo decline | Segment B conversation | Mention QUIZ only if live; otherwise use newsletter. |
| May 23 | Newsletter #1 | Subscribe + soft conversion | Use two CTAs only if both lead magnets are live. |

---

## 6. Weekly Review

After 7 days, record:

- Profile visits:
- Qualified comments:
- DM conversations started:
- Newsletter signups:
- Audit requests:
- Quiz requests:
- Demos booked:
- Top post by qualified conversation:
- Weakest post and why:

After 14 days:

- `[ ]` Keep 5 posts/week because quality and conversations are holding.
- `[ ]` Reduce to 3 posts/week because sourcing, drafting, or DM handling is slipping.
- `[ ]` Redirect Segment B CTA to newsletter until quiz is ready.
- `[ ]` Tighten toward Segment A if demos are the priority and conversations are too broad.
