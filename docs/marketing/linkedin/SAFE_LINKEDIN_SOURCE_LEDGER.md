## Publication Rule

Use exact numbers only when `Confidence` is `approved` or `usable-with-caution` and the post wording matches the approved wording.

If a number is `hold`, either:

- remove the number,
- soften to a directional statement,
- or find and retain the primary source before scheduling.

Never cite "Barreau", "Loi 25", "ABA", "Clio", "Cadieux", "NALP", or "Thomson Reuters" in a post unless the exact claim is present in this ledger.
